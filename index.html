Your Personal Sedona Retreat at ... Casa Ananda

Come and enjoy the wonder and magic of Sedona in a very unique way— stay in your own Casa, which means “small house” in Spanish. Casa Ananda is located in West Sedona a few blocks from beautiful hiking trails and parklands and only minutes from restaurants and shops. Enjoy the spectacular rooftop deck view, where you can watch the morning rays gently illumine world famous Coffeepot Rock, Thunder Mountain, and Chimney Rock.

Casa Ananda retreat offers you an intimate “local” experience of Sedona away from the busy, tourist-y hotels. Casa Ananda is located in a quiet residential area of West Sedona, so you can deeply immerse yourself in the beauty and healing power that is Red Rock Country.

Casa Ananda is designed to support your physical well-being by providing you with a healthy, green environment, including water filtration throughout the Casa, non-toxic paint, and formaldehyde-free materials.

You can rent Casa Ananda for $160 per night or combine it with various options: guided hiking, Meditation Classes, or enjoy Halotherapy in the Salt Cabin.

Settle in and find the true heart of Sedona, you’ll find your own self.
