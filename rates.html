Policies

Minimum Stay

A three night minimum stay is required.

Room Deposit

A 1 night deposit will be taken at the time of reservation with the credit card provided. The balance of the stay is due at check-in and payable with Credit Card, Travelers checks or Cash.

Smoking

This is a non-smoking, drug-free and alcohol-free facility.

Pets

Sorry, no pets allowed. For local boarding, please call Bark N Purr at 928-282-4108.

Cancellations

A 14-day cancellation notice is required for no charge to occur. If sufficient notice is not given then the deposit of a one-night stay is charged.
