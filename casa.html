About Casa Ananda

Welcome to your own personal retreat, with all of the amenities of home amidst the red rocks. Enjoy the bright and colorful living room with a pull-out couch, fully equipped modern kitchenette, travertine tiled bathroom with shower, and a serene bedroom with a queen-size bed.

The Casa has many outdoor spaces for you to enjoy. Just off the kitchen, surrounded by greenery, is a private patio for your morning coffee. On many mornings, the resident quail scurry by with their babies trailing behind. Just off the bedroom, the rooftop deck has a shaded, comfortable seating area on one side and, on the other, a bed to lie back and view Sedonaâ€™s sparkling, star-filled night sky. The deck even has room to set up a yoga mat (provided) for your early morning practice, as the sun slowly illuminates Red Rock Country around you. A stunning and wonderful spot!

Casa Ananda is a 600 square foot self-contained building connected to the main house through the garage and is completely private from the main house.

You can explore Sedona on your own or enjoy some of the Casa Ananda options available to you; hikes to sacred spots unknown to the general public, Halotherapy in the Salt Spa, or meditation classes.

This is your time for rest, renewal, and transformation. Casa Ananda offers this to you in a beautiful, sweet, and delight-filled way.
